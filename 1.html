The Unseen Terror of Allergies

In the spring, flowers bloom咲く and beautiful blossoms開花 can be seen all across Japan. Isesaki’s Mirai (Future) Park is well known for the beautiful cherry blossoms you can see in March and April. Plum blossoms are another popular tree in the spring. They are slightly different than cherry blossoms and give off a different aroma. They also bloom earlier than cherry blossoms.

But the spring season also brings allergiesアレルギー . In Japan, allergies are very apparent明らかな . Over 40% of people have watery eyes, runny noses, coughing, and lots of sneezing in the spring. These allergies are known as hay fever花粉症 and are mostly caused by cedar and cypress pollen花粉 .

Allergies occur発生する (happen) when the immune system免疫系 reacts to～に反応する a foreign異物 substance物質 inside the body. The immune system makes antibodies抗体 that attack things such as germs細菌 , but in the case of allergies, the body makes antibodies even though the allergensアレルゲン　（アレルギーの原因となる物質） are not harmful有害 . This leads to～の原因となる the symptoms症状 which occur.

In Japan, people commonly wear face masks as a way to fight allergies. You can also avoid避ける outdoor areas during the day at times when pollen is known to be worse. And some people take special medications薬 (medicine) or use eye drops. Spring is a beautiful time of year, but all beautiful things come at a cost犠牲を伴う .

The Japanese cedar杉 covers Japan in pollen from February to April. The pollen from the cedar tree is very small and can travel long distances. It can even travel to other countries!

It's an important tree and is used for building many things, so people planted many, many cedar trees. But now it causes one of the biggest and worst allergies in Japan.

The Japanese cypressヒノキ is another very important tree in Japan. Its wood is used for temples and shrines because it is strong and durable. It is also used to make furniture and other things. The wood has a very nice smell, and it is said to be good for your health.

Pollen from the cypress tree also causes allergies. Its pollen season usually starts a little later than the cedar tree's, so allergy season lasts for a longer time.

Grasses and weeds草・雑草 are other common causes of allergies. But it's very hard to control these plants. They grow everywhere, and they are very strong. They can even grow in the cracks of sidewalks!

Actually, rice and wheat are kinds of grasses, too. But they are not usually a problem for allergies. The pollen from these grasses is too heavy to travel very far.

Dust mitesダニ are a big cause of allergies, especially in the winter! They are actually very small bugs, and they can go everywhere in your house. Dust mites are very small, so you can't see them. But you can feel them! They can make your skin itchy and red, too.

They especially love bedding and tatami rooms. And actually, can cause very serious allergies, so clean your house often! they are not dangerous at all. But they can cause allergies in some people.
