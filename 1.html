About Hanukkah

Hanukkah is a special Jewish holiday that lasts eight nights. A long time ago, there was a miracle in a Jewish temple. People had only a little bit of oil, but it burned for eight days!

Now, people light candles on a special stand called a menorah. They add one more candle each night. Families eat yummy fried foods like potato pancakes (latkes) and donuts (sufganiyot). Kids play a game with a spinning top called a dreidel. Sometimes, they get small gifts too!

Hanukkah is a nice time to be with family!
