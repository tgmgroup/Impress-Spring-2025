About This Issue

Hello, this is George, and thanks for reading this Spring, 2025 issue of Impress.

In this issue, we celebrate the hidden things of spring: the pollen in the air that causes our allergies and the bugs around us that we don't think about. And we have some great puzzles from Nick for you to solve.

Because this issue is about hidden things, we want to remind you that our lives are not only the things we see and think about. For example, we spend a lot of our days in schools, doing homework, or using our phones, but do you spend a lot of time folding clothes, washing dishes, or cleaning the house? If you think about it, there are a lot of people doing things for us all the time. Spring is the season for Mother's Day and Father's Day, so remember to say thank you to the people you care about for all the good things that they do.

We hope you enjoy reading this issue, and we hope to hear what you think about it! We'd love to hear your comments and ideas for the next issue.
